About

Supreme Sprinkler Solutions brings you a supreme solution to your water wasting. Our system fully automates your sprinkler system according to your local weather. Your sprinklers will no longer run when its raining and will turn on when your lawn actually needs it. After a simple installation you will be able to view your sprinkler's automatic schedule from anywhere using any internet connected device. In addition, our web app will allow you to view the weather and customize your lawn zones and the watering schedule. Additionally, our system, attached near your sprinkler controller, will have a 6.5" touch screen to enable you to perform all these actions at your home.
