Server Setup

You're halfway to having a watered lawn! Follow these two steps to finish:

1) Turn on your Raspberry Pi ADEM System by plugging in the Raspberry Pi's power source.
2) After it boots up, either click the 'Setup' icon on the desktop or open up the Chromium browser to begin the Raspberry Pi setup.

Once you're done with the setup, to view the updated web-app pages refresh this page or logout and log back in.
